Features

Screen Time Management

Set time limits to ensure balanced screen usage.

The Screen Time Management feature allows parents to set personalized time limits for their children's device usage. This ensures a balanced approach to screen time, helping to prevent excessive use of devices and promoting healthier habits. Parents can define specific daily or weekly limits for individual apps or overall device usage, providing control over how much time their children spend on entertainment, gaming, or social media. The dashboard provides real-time tracking and alerts when time limits are approached or exceeded, allowing parents to intervene when necessary. This feature supports a healthier digital lifestyle for children by encouraging a balance between screen time and offline activities.

Content Filtering

Block inappropriate content and ensure a safe online experience.

The Content Filtering feature allows parents to control and restrict access to inappropriate or harmful content on their children's devices. With customizable filters, parents can block websites, apps, or online content based on categories such as violence, adult themes, or explicit language. The dashboard enables real-time monitoring and updates to ensure that the content being accessed aligns with age-appropriate standards. Parents can also set specific restrictions for different age groups or individual children, ensuring tailored protection. This feature enhances online safety by preventing exposure to harmful material and providing peace of mind for parents regarding their children's digital experience.
